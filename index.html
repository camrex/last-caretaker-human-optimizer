Welcome

Plan and track your Hall of Humanity workflow

This app helps you optimize food and memories, model session and rocket plans, track profession progress, and run a second-screen phone companion while playing fullscreen.

What is available
OptimizerBuild the best food + memory plan for a profession, with inventory and session-aware constraints.
SandboxTest custom quantities, inspect resulting traits, and preview likely satisfied professions.
PlanOrganize sessions, assign rockets, identify per-session and global crafting deficits, and share read-only snapshots.
Second-screen QR shareGenerate a QR code and open a mobile-focused read-only plan page on your phone so your game can stay fullscreen on PC.
ProgressTrack planned/created professions and committee completion in one view.
Reference tabsBrowse professions, foods, and memories while editing inventory directly from cards.
DataExport and import your planning profile to back up and restore local state.
Suggested flow
1. Use Optimizer or Sandbox to design candidate humans.
2. Add them to Plan and review session shortages before crafting.
3. Click Share plan via QR and scan with your phone for a read-only mobile snapshot while you play fullscreen.
4. Track completion in Progress and periodically back up through Data.
